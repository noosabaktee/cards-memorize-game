How to play

  1. There are 3 phases to this challenge. Memorize, Recall and Examine
  2. In the Memorize phase, users will be given 30 minutes to remember the cards displayed on the screen in the correct order.
  3. At the Recall stage, users will be invited to arrange the cards into the card holder and will only be given 15 minutes
  4. At the final stage Examine, the cards that have been arranged into the card holder will be proven
  5. In the Memorize and Recall phase the user can press a button to move to the next phase even though the time has not yet run out
